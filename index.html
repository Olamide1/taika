Frequently Asked Questions

What does the package include?
Our package covers the entire development process. You get a fully functional MVP, including initial design, front-end and back-end development with Vanilla JS, NodeJS/Express, and MySQL, all within 4-6 weeks.
How does the development process work?
Share your idea and v1 feature list with us. We'll start with design mockups and move on to development after your approval. Regular updates and your feedback are integral parts of our process until your MVP is ready to launch.
What if my MVP needs more than 6 weeks?
Our goal is to deliver within 4-6 weeks. If your MVP requires more time due to complexity or added features, we'll communicate this early on. Any additional work will be discussed and agreed upon beforehand.
Can you support further development after the MVP launch?
Absolutely! We're here for the long haul. Post-launch, we offer further development, enhancements, and support packages tailored to your needs and budget.
