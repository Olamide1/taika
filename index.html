Frequently Asked Questions

What does the $3k package include?

For $3k, you get a fully functional MVP of your product, designed and developed in 4-6 weeks. This includes initial design, front-end and back-end development with Vanilla JS, NodeJS/Express, and MySQL, and deployment.

How does the development process work?

Once you share your idea and v1 feature list, we'll start with design mockups. After your approval, we move on to development. You'll receive regular updates, and we incorporate your feedback iteratively until the MVP is ready to launch.

What if my MVP needs more than 6 weeks?

Our aim is to deliver within 4-6 weeks. If your MVP requires more time due to complexity or added features, we'll communicate this early in the process. Additional work may involve extra costs, which we'll discuss and agree upon beforehand.

Can you support further development after the MVP launch?

Yes! We're here to support your growth. After your MVP launch, we can discuss further development, enhancements, and support packages tailored to your needs and budget.
